# Contributing to `mapepire`

Thanks for your interest in contributing! The full contributor guide
lives in [`AGENTS.md`](AGENTS.md) — coding standards, testing
requirements, the pre-PR checklist, security invariants, and the
donation-readiness checklist are all there.

## Quick links

- [`AGENTS.md`](AGENTS.md) — contributor & AI-assistant guide (canonical)
- [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) — vulnerability reporting policy
- [`Makefile`](Makefile) — `make help` lists all targets

## Before you open a PR

1. `make pre-pr` — runs format-check, clippy, all tests, audit, deny, doc
2. Update `CHANGELOG.md` under `[Unreleased]`
3. Fill in every section of the PR template

By submitting a pull request, you agree to license your contribution
under both [MIT](LICENSE-MIT) and [Apache-2.0](LICENSE-APACHE).
